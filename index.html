Government Initiative

Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan

Building public knowledge infrastructure for Maharashtra's socio-economic and cultural development, especially for the bottom 80% of the population.

UMA is a project of the Ministry of Higher and Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra, aligned with the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA) of the Government of India. It creates a formal mechanism for local bodies like Gram Panchayats and Zilla Parishads to access knowledge institutions including IITs and engineering colleges for their development needs.
