Workshops & Training

Capacity building programs, research methods training, and community engagement workshops organized by the Rural Research Group.

2024
15 Dec 2024

Sustainable Agriculture Practices for Small Farmers

Community Hall, Bundelkhand 2 days

A two-day capacity building workshop focused on climate-resilient farming techniques, organic pest management, and soil health improvement. Over 50 farmers from 12 villages participated in hands-on training sessions and field demonstrations.

28 Nov 2024

Research Methods in Rural Studies

University Campus 5 days

An intensive five-day training program for graduate students and early-career researchers on qualitative and quantitative methods for rural research. Topics included survey design, participatory rural appraisal, and ethical considerations.

10 Oct 2024

Water Conservation and Harvesting Techniques

Watershed Development Office, Marathwada 1 day

Community workshop on traditional and modern water harvesting methods. Participants learned about rainwater collection, groundwater recharge, and efficient irrigation practices. Included site visits to successful water conservation projects.

22 Sep 2024

Women's Self-Help Group Leadership Training

District Rural Development Agency, Rayalaseema 3 days

Leadership and enterprise development training for women SHG leaders. Sessions covered financial management, market linkages, conflict resolution, and group dynamics. 35 women leaders from across the district participated.

05 Aug 2024

GIS and Remote Sensing for Rural Planning

University Computer Lab 3 days

Technical workshop on using geospatial tools for rural development planning. Participants learned to analyze satellite imagery, create resource maps, and use GIS software for decision support. Targeted at researchers and government officers.

18 Jul 2024

Agricultural Reforms and Farmer Welfare

State Planning Commission 1 day

Stakeholder consultation bringing together policymakers, researchers, farmer representatives, and civil society organizations. Discussed implementation challenges and recommendations for agricultural policy reforms.

02 Jun 2024

Traditional Knowledge Documentation Workshop

Tribal Research Centre, Jharkhand 4 days

Training on methods for documenting indigenous knowledge systems in agriculture and natural resource management. Emphasized ethical protocols, community consent, and intellectual property rights of traditional knowledge holders.

15 Apr 2024

Climate Change Adaptation Strategies

Agricultural Extension Office, Vidarbha 2 days

Farmer awareness program on climate change impacts and adaptation options. Featured presentations on weather-based crop advisories, drought-tolerant varieties, and crop insurance schemes. Interactive sessions with scientists.

08 Feb 2024

Participatory Rural Appraisal Training

University Campus 5 days

Foundational training on PRA tools and techniques for field researchers. Covered transect walks, resource mapping, seasonal calendars, Venn diagrams, and ranking exercises. Included field practice in nearby villages.

12 Jan 2024

Natural Farming Techniques Workshop

Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Andhra Pradesh 2 days

Hands-on training on zero-budget natural farming methods. Farmers learned to prepare biofertilizers, botanical pesticides, and mulching techniques. Field visits to successful natural farming plots were organized.
